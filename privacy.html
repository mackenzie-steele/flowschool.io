Privacy note

Your work is yours.

When you make an account, we keep your email, your password (encrypted — even we can't read it), your profile, and everything you create here: classes, flows, stories, playlists, saved prompts. All of it lives in Supabase, a secure database. The site runs on Vercel, which counts visits without cookies and without following you around the internet. If you send us feedback or something breaks, we keep those notes too — page, browser, the technical bits — so we can fix it. Nobody sees your work but you. We can reach it because we run the database, and we hold that like the trust it is. We will never sell it. Never show you ads. Never share a thing. Want a copy? Download everything, anytime (Saved → Export backup). Want out? Delete your account (Profile → Delete account) and it's gone. Really gone. This is a beta — everything is an experiment, including this app — so if something ever feels off, tell us: support@flowschool.io. We're real people. We'll answer.

— Bonnie & Mackenzie
