Your Cues

A clear cue has three parts: an action, body part, and direction of movement. When cued in this order, students can better understand what you’re saying and feel like you’re speaking directly to them. Write your flow, then fill out the worksheet with the directions to guide that flow. Save it and practice saying it out loud before you’re in front of a class.
